Ingredients
1 Butter
1 Sugar
1 Semisweet Chocolate
1 Vanilla Extract
7 Egg
1 Cream of Tartar
1 Water
---
Instructions
Butter the baking dish
Coat the baking dish with sugar
Combine chocolate, vanilla, and water
Stir egg yolk into chocolate mixture
Beat egg whites and cream of tartar
Fold whipped egg whites into chocolate mixture
Add the mixture into the baking dish
