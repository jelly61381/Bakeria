Ingredients
1 Elbow Pasta
1 Butter
1 AP Flour
1 Whole Milk
1 Half and Half
1 Shredded Medium Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 Shredded Gruyere Cheese
1 Salt
1 Black Pepper
1 Paprika
---
Instructions
Boil the Elbow Pasta
Mix Shredded Medium Sharp Cheddar Cheese and Shredded Gruyere Cheese
Melt Butter
Whisk AP Flour
Whisk Half and Half
Whisk Whole Milk
Combine mixture with the Elbow Pasta
Sprinkle the cheese mixture on top of the pasta
Bake the dish
